About Us

We are a well established car detailing business in Moyock, North Carolina. We make the trip to your residence and do interior and exterior details along with whatever else your car needs. We do our best to work efficiently and effectively as to get your vehicle back to you as soon as possible.

Our ultimate goal is to grow this business into one of the most reliable and trusted detailing shops in the Albemarle. Whether working on a luxury vehicle or an everyday commuter car, our commitment to excellence ensures that every customer leaves satisfied with the quality and care they receive.
